

1. Introduction: Introduce the main characters and their world. Explain the theme of the comic book and what readers can expect from the story.

2. Conflict: Establish the conflict between the characters and their world. Introduce any antagonists or obstacles that will stand in the way of the characters.

3. Rising Action: As the characters struggle with the conflict, the stakes become higher and the tension builds.

4. Climax: The characters’ fates are determined as they confront the conflict head-on.

5. Resolution: The characters’ victory or defeat is revealed, and the consequences of their actions are explored.

6. Epilogue: The characters’ journey is wrapped up with a conclusion that ties up any loose ends.

7. Afterword: The creator of the comic book reflects on the story and its themes.
